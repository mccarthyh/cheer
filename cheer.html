About
    Join the walpole Highschool cheer team if you love to tumble and stunt, or even if you have any interest in the sport! We have a Competition team for anyone who wants to compete their routine with other local towns. Anyone who is inexperienced to the sport, or is not interested in competing a routine can join the sideline team, this is where you only have to cheer at games. Everyone makes the sideline team.
Tryouts
    The first round of evalutions take place in early June. These are more like evaluations than tryouts, just so the Coach can see what each athlete can do. These evaluations take place at Brodricks Gymnastics at the Walpole Mall.
    Competition team tryouts are the last week of August in the WHS gym. This is where the coach decides who makes the Competition tean and who does not.
Schedule
    Practices start the last week of August, and they end the last week of November.
    Each week, Competition team practices are Monday-Wednesday from 2:45pm-5:00pm in the gym.
    Game Day practices take place every Thurdsay from 2:45pm-5:00pm
